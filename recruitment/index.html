Recruitment Process

1. PA will organize briefing meeting, introducing specific departmental information and sharing experience to freshmen.

2. The candidate should submit application materials through the application link, and after 11:59 will close the entry channel.

3. The first round is based on resume to select, and this section is organized by the Human Resource department. They will eliminate the candidate who does not meet the basic requirements of Panda Association. The elimination number shouldn’t exceed 5% of the enrollment.

4. After receiving the interview e-mail, the candidate should add the interview time which they can participate in and the first two department they would like to apply. What’s more, the person who has not filled in the form is considered to give up automatically.

5. The second round is case interview directed by the specific department.
The interview concludes three sections:

a) Self-introduction.
b) Case interview (which is designed by specific work situation, in order to investigate whether the interviewer is suitable for the department's work characteristics). Administration Department: We are looking for members who are attentive and good at planning. So, the case will be a designed circumstance with multiple tasks needed to be finished in the meantime or in a short period. This is a test to see how the person will behave and plan under such pressure. Publicity Department: We are looking for the member who has great communication skills. The case need the candidate to contact with local business for sponsorship of major activity. Human Resource Department: we are looking for sensible and sensitive members. Case: if confliction happens between two department, how the candidates deal with this friction in the shortest time. Activity Department: We are looking for hard-working guys with a flexible mind. Case: set up an artificial mistake during the activity process, in order to test how they will do to remedy.
c) Question and answer time (Q&A).

6. The interview results should be announced in one week and should send an email notification to both the candidate and the person who is not been selected. After the candidate passed the second round, the HR department will arrange them according to their desire and the number limit from every department (first wishes priority).
